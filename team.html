Our Research Team

Meet the interdisciplinary team of researchers behind the geospatial analysis of COVID-19 vaccination coverage in Kenya, bringing together expertise in spatial epidemiology, data science, public health, and health equity.

Lead Researchers

Dr. Diego F. Cuadros

Principal Investigator

Digital Epidemiology Laboratory, University of Cincinnati

Dr. Cuadros specializes in spatial epidemiology and geospatial analysis of infectious diseases. His research focuses on identifying geographic patterns of disease prevalence and the structural determinants driving health disparities in low- and middle-income countries.

Larry Niño

Lead Author & Spatial Epidemiologist

Digital Epidemiology Laboratory, University of Cincinnati

Larry specializes in computational epidemiology and the application of machine learning methods to public health challenges. His research focuses on identifying spatial determinants of health outcomes in resource-limited settings using advanced geospatial analysis techniques.
